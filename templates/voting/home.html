How It Works

1
Admin Creates a Vote

Admins set up a voting session by entering names for Prepared Speakers, Evaluators, and Table Topics Speakers.

2
Members Vote

Members visit the unique voting link and cast their votes for their favorite performers in each category.

3
Admin Closes Polls

When voting concludes, the admin closes the polls and the winners are displayed.

4
Results Announcement

Winners are announced for each category, with vote counts visible only to admins for a brief moment.
